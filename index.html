How do I get help on Instagram?

1. Start the Instagram app on your iPhone or Android device and tap your profile picture in the bottom-right corner. 2. Tap the three-line menu at the top-right corner, and then select Settings. 3. Tap Help, then tap Help Center. Clicking Help Center will take you to Instagram's Help Center pages.
